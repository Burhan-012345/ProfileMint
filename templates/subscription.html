Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?

Our premium plan is a monthly subscription that gives you unlimited access to all premium features. You can cancel anytime with no obligation.

Will I lose access to my resumes if I cancel?

No, you won't lose access to your resumes. You'll still be able to view, edit, and download any resumes you created while you were a premium member. However, you won't be able to create new resumes using premium templates after cancellation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal for payments.

Can I switch between free and premium plans?

Yes, you can upgrade to premium at any time, and you can also cancel your premium subscription to return to the free plan at the end of your billing cycle.
